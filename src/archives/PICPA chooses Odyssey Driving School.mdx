**The Philippine Institute of Certified Public Accountants (PICPA)-CDO Chapter**,led by its President, Ms. Gladys Jill S. Cua, partners with top companies in the city. It recently launched a privilege program. Members of this program enjoy discounted rates from business establishments selected for quality service, excellence and integrity.

Under the driving school category, PICPA chooses Odyssey Driving School as its sole partner. Certified Public Accountants (CPAs) can avail of discounted rates once they enroll in Odyssey Driving School.

On July 21, 2010, PICPA formally entered a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) with Odyssey Driving School and other business firms. The MOA was signed was held at Pearlmonth Hotel at Limketkai Drive, Cagayan de Oro City. The ceremony seals the partnership between the two institutions which are publicly known for their integrity, passion and excellence.
